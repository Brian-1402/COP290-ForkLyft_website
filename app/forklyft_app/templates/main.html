Where it all began

The ForkLyft Story

In B33, Aravali Hostel, four friends with an insatiable passion for food came together to create Forklyft. Utkarsh, the master chef; Pratham, the coding whiz; Brian, the discerning food critic; and Hardik, the creative food photographer. Driven by their shared love for all things culinary, they vowed to create a platform that would revolutionize the way people experienced food.

Countless sleepless nights and relentless hard work later, Forklyft was born. With a tagline that said, You won't be able to resist lifting your forks, Forklyft quickly gained traction as a website that hosted a curated collection of restaurants, each with its unique story and culinary masterpiece. Their innovative features, personalized recommendations, and seamless ordering process brought the joy of dining out right to users' doorsteps.

Despite challenges and setbacks, the four founders remained steadfast in their vision. Forklyft grew by leaps and bounds, introducing live cooking classes, exclusive food events, and a charity initiative to support local food communities. Today, Forklyft stands tall as a beacon of culinary excellence, a platform that has changed the way people experience food.

As users lift their forks to indulge in the delectable offerings on Forklyft, they are reminded of the tagline that started it all - a testament to the unwavering determination and passion of four friends who dared to dream big and create a culinary revolution with Forklyft.
